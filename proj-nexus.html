Project Description:
Nexus Films is a web-app I developed that allows user to interact with their favorite films all in one place. The site offers a detailed review process, breaking ratings down into 5 unique categories. Users can save their favorites to a list, where they can sort and rank films. Reviews and lists can be shared between groups of users, so you can see what other are watching and even create group watchlists.
