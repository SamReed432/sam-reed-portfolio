Project Description:
Over the past seven years, I've designed and programmed lighting for a wide range of theatrical productions at venues including The Shoreline Theater Academy and Tufts University's Cohen Auditorium. My work includes black box plays, full-scale musicals, and experimental student showcases, with a focus on cue-heavy designs executed using ETC Element, Ion, and Nomad systems. Across these projects, I've led everything from initial plot creation to hands-on programming and live operation, combining technical fluency with a collaborative, storytelling-driven design approach.
