About Me

Nexus

Nexus Films is a web-app I developed that allows users to interact with their favorite films all in one place. The site offers a detailed review process, breaking ratings down into 5 unique categories. Users can save their favorites to a list, where they can sort and rank films. Reviews and lists can be shared between groups of users, so you can see what other are watching and even create group watchlists.

Reed Family Light Show

The Reed Family Light Show started in 2014 and has grown into a shorline tradition. The show features over 10,000 programmable rgb pixels synchronized to music though our radio station. This construction of the project includes fully customed programmed songs, and custom built contollers that communicate over a local network with a series of Raspberry Pis to control the show.

Cookbook +

As a final project for Web Development, I worked with a team to create this full-stack web-app. Cookbook+ allowed users to create accounts, browse and filter recipes, and create there own cookbook. This site used Node.JS on the backend to communicate with recipe APIs and store user information in MongoDB.
